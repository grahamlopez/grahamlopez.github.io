
*******************
Project navigation
*******************

One of the defining features of vim is the modal editing, one of which is
largely concerned with navigating within a buffer quickly. However, there is
also the matter of multiple-file and project-wide navigation to consider.

- built-ins
    - location history, location list, marks, bookmarks
    - :e, :ls/:b, :find, :argadd
- tag jumping
- nerdtree
- vimgrep, ripgrep, fzf, ag
- semantic jumping
